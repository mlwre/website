mlw.re (2012) is a private non-profit organisation composed by security professionals and focused on the research of malicious software (malware) and its associated practices: reverse engineering, forensics, threat intelligence, incident response, etc.

Members come from different backgrounds have a lot of expertise in different areas of the information security field. Members are expected to collaborate with both knowledge and other resources (hardware, development, organisation, etc) in order to accomplish projects driven by and for the mlw.re community.

We are a continuously growing community of over 100 members whose hard work and dedication allowed us to achieve completion in projects in areas such as:

  • Malware analysis
  • Botnets tracking
  • Intelligence sharing
  • New Threats
  • Malware Classification
  • DFIR
